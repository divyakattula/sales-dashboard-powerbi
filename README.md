# 📊 Sales Overview Dashboard — Power BI

> Analyzing $89M in global sales across countries, customers, and managers to surface actionable business insights.

## 📌 Problem Statement
A retail business needed a centralized view of sales performance across multiple countries, customer types, and sales managers — with no existing reporting system in place.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total sales, cost, and profit at a glance
- Identify top-performing countries and customers
- Compare manager performance across the team
- Analyze year-on-year sales trends


## 📂 Dataset
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Total Records | Multi-country sales data |
| Period | 2023 – 2024 |
| Tools | Power BI, DAX, Excel |


## 🔍 Key Insights
1. **USA** leads all markets at $38M — 43% of total revenue
2. **Supermarkets** drive 56% of sales by customer type
3. **Union Co-Op & Whole Foods** together account for 23% of revenue
4. Sales dropped **22% YoY** (2023: $50M → 2024: $39M) — needs investigation
5. **Rajiv** leads manager performance at $16.2M vs $5.5M (Oliver) — 3x gap


## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (data modeling, DAX measures, visualizations)
- Excel / CSV (data source)
- DAX (KPI calculations)
